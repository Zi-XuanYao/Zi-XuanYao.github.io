About

I am a PhD student at the University of the Sunshine Coast working on Finance research supervised by Dr. Hammad Siddiqi and Prof. Sajid Anwar. I hold a matster's degree at the University College Dublin, Ireland and worked at the Chinese headquarter of the United Overseas Bank, Singapore before joining the research group. Meanwhile, I contribute to the Certified Fintech Analyst Institute, America, WorldQuant and some small businesses with a part-time consultant role and hold memberships in several international professional institutes and industry associations. Besides, my professional qualifications contain the Analyst of Quantitative Finance (AQF), Certified FinTech Analyst (CFTA), Certified Green Finance Consultant (CGFC), China Intermediate Qualification Certificate of Banking Professional (QCBP), and etc.
